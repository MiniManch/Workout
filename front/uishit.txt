

Coach Creation and Editing:

Ability to create and edit coach profiles.
Client Creation, Editing, and Deletion:

Coaches can add, edit, and delete clients. The coach manages their own clients.
Session Creation, Update, and Deletion:

Includes assigning clients to sessions. Coaches can create, update, and delete training sessions, and assign clients to them.
Screen to View All Clients of a Coach:

Display all clients assigned to a coach, including their training history. Each client should show their last training date.
Screen to View All Sessions of a Coach:

A view of all sessions scheduled by the coach, resembling a calendar view.(GUI emphasis is not required)
These translations should provide a clearer understanding of the system's functionalities in managing coaches, clients, and training sessions for a CrossFit coach.
